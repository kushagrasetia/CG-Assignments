About The Book :

1.) This book covers many topics related to the “art of living.”
2.) The authors define ikigai and the rules of ikigai; they conducted a total of one hundred interviews in Ogimi, Okinawa to try to understand the longevity secrets of centenarians and supercentenarians.
3.) “What do Japanese artisans, engineers, Zen philosophy, and cuisine have in common? Simplicity and attention to detail.”
4.) “The authors of this book wish you a long, happy, and purposeful life.”
